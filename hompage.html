About Us

E-Gram Panchayat is a digital initiative to make village-level services more accessible, faster, and transparent for rural citizens. This platform allows villagers to easily apply for essential services such as Birth Certificates, Water Connections, Aadhaar Card updates, and Ration Cards — all from the comfort of their home or local center.

  • Simplify the application process
  • Reduce paperwork and delays
  • Allow users to track their application status online
  • Empower panchayat staff and officers to manage requests efficiently

Our goal is to bridge the gap between technology and rural governance, ensuring that every citizen receives timely services without the need to stand in long queues or visit multiple offices.

"Digital India starts from the village – and E-Gram Panchayat is a step toward that future."
