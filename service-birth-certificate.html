E-Gram Panchayat

Birth Certificate Service

A Birth Certificate is one of the most important identity documents in a person's life. It provides legal proof of birth, name, date of birth, place of birth, and parentage. It is essential for school admissions, obtaining an Aadhaar card, applying for a passport, claiming inheritance rights, or availing of various government schemes and services. Traditionally, villagers had to physically visit the Gram Panchayat office to apply for a birth certificate, often dealing with long queues, missing documents, delays, and the hassle of multiple visits.

With the E-Gram Panchayat digital platform, the process has become extremely simple and accessible. Villagers no longer need to travel or wait for days just to submit a form. By simply visiting the website, users can create an account through the Sign Up option using their email and a password. Once registered, they can Sign In and access their personal dashboard, where all available services are neatly listed, including the option to Apply for a Birth Certificate.

The application form is clean and easy to fill, requiring basic details like the child's name, date of birth, gender, parent details, and the permanent address. The user then submits the form with just one click. The data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore, and a unique application ID is created. Users can view their submitted applications anytime under the "My Applications" section, where they can also check the status of their request — whether it's Pending, Approved, or Rejected — without visiting the Panchayat office.

On the other side, Panchayat staff and officers can access these applications through their admin panel and review, verify, and update statuses in real time. This transparent and paperless system ensures faster processing and zero loss of records. The E-Gram Panchayat portal saves time, improves accuracy, and builds trust in governance. Applying for a birth certificate, which once took days, can now be done in under 5 minutes from any mobile or computer with internet access — truly bringing Digital India to the grassroots.

  • Submit your application online
  • Upload supporting documents (proof of birth, parent ID, etc.)
  • Track your application status
  • Receive your certificate digitally or collect it from your local center
